MOLLY BROWN

ABOUT

Molly Brown is an independent artist from Berkshire, releasing her first EP ‘Forever Guessing’ at the age of 20. Molly has been playing the piano and singing from a young age. Ever since, she has enjoyed putting her own spin on covering songs and performing at a wide range of events and venues. Whilst at University, Molly thoroughly enjoyed working and performing with different bands on lead vocals. Furthermore, songwriting has always been an outlet for Molly. From writing and recording demos in her bedroom, Molly has since worked with a producer, who has brought her ideas to life and helped to make it possible for her to release some music. She has thorougly enjoyed the creative process so far and is looking forward to releasing new music very soon.

RELEASES

What You Couldn't Give To Me

I released my second single ‘What You Couldn’t Give To Me’ in May 2025. I wrote this track after a negative experience and desperately wanted to experiment with these lyrics to create some sarcasm. The overall message is that feeling when you realise you never really know someone which I’m sure many of my listeners can relate to. I have been so grateful for my track to have been played on BBC Introducing, Future Hits Radio and Kennet Radio. My track also climbed to the Top 10 on the Future Hits Radio Top 40 which was a massive achievement.

Forever Guessing

I released my first single ‘Gone Away’ in September 2024 which was followed by the release of my EP ‘Forever Guessing’ in October 2024. The four tracks represent different stages and emotions felt during heartbreak. I hope my music resonates with my listeners and echoes important messages and emotions. Working with my producer Martin Pawley, I thoroughly enjoyed the creative process and watching my initial ideas come to life. I was fortunate enough to have my fourth track ‘Switch Off’ feature on BBC Introducing which extremely exciting.
